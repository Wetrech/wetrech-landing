What We Build

SMART STREET LIGHTING SYSTEM

◈
Retrofit Module

Plug-and-play device that fits existing lamp sockets. No new wiring required. Installable in under 30 seconds per lamp.

◉
Self-Healing Mesh Network

Lamps communicate wirelessly and form a self-healing network — if one node fails, the mesh reroutes automatically. No single point of failure.

◐
Central Dashboard

Real-time fault detection, remote dimming control, energy analytics and scheduling — all from one web platform accessible by municipalities.

⬡
Fault Detection

Sensors detect lamp failures instantly. Maintenance teams are notified before citizens even notice. Eliminate unnecessary field visits.

◈
Smart Dimming

Automatic brightness scheduling based on time, traffic and ambient light. Up to 40% energy reduction with zero impact on public safety.

◉
Emergency Alert System

Lamps can serve as city-wide alert nodes — visual signals, audio warnings and custom notifications pushed instantly across the entire network.
